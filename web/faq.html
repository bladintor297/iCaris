Frequently Asked Questions

Where are we located?

We are located at Skudai, Kulai, Pasir Gudang and Johor Bahru area.

How much the rental rate?

Depends on the car type, it varies from RM5/hour until RM40/hour.

Do you have student price?

Yes of course. We do have special discount for students, especially UTM Skudai students.

Can I rent a car if I only have B class license?

No you cannot. You must at least have P Driving License of minimum DA class.
